Eligibility


  • He/She should secure at least C+ in SEE.
  • He/She should pass Entrance Examination under supervision of SNSBS.

Entrance Test


  • For Science Stream, Entrance Test will be based on Mathematics, English & Science subjects.
  • For Management Stream, Entrance Test will be based on Mathematics, English, Computer and G.K.
  • Entrance Test will be taken in multiple choice system.
  • The candidates must appear and pass Entrance Test conducted by the institution.

Entrance Result


  • The Entrance result of the successful candidates for admission will be published on the basis of SLC score & Entrance score.
  • The candidates must face interview along with parents at the time of admission.


Scholarship Provisions


Saraswati Niketan provides significant number of scholarship to academically sound and financially needy students under several titles. Deserving students may obtain details of scholarship schemes from the Saraswati Niketan administration.


Scholarship Schemes


    1. Grade XI

    • 3.8 above in SEE- 100% discount in admission & 100% discount in monthly fee
    • 3.6-3.8 in SEE- 50% discount in admission & 25% discount in monthly fee
    • 3.2-3.6 -25% discount in admission fee
    • Entrance topper - 1st :3 months fee – 2nd: 2 months fee – 3rd 1 month fee
    • Term Topper -Running term tuition fee.
    • First come first serve in admission in XI (10 student)- 25% admission free.
    • A wide range of scholarship to deserving & needy students under the categories of:
      -Financially poor(Must be convincing)
      -Remote area
      -Martyrs’ Family
      -Disables
      -Under privileged group

    2. Grade XII

    • National topper in XI-100% discount in admission & 100% discount in monthly fee
    • Class XI topper in school-100% discount in admission & 100% discount in monthly fee
