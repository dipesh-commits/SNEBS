About us

Located in a serene environment at Jajuwa, Gaindakot, Saraswati Niketan Secondary Boarding School (SNSBS) is an English medium co-education institution established in 2050B.S. The college offers Science, Management with computer science, Hotel Management & Education programs designed to follow the curriculum of National Education Board (NEB) of Nepal. The main aim of SNSBS is to provide world class education by instilling good values and passion for what our students set out to achieve in the outer world. Our faculty is attuned to the needs of each student and supplements class lectures with tutorials, laboratory work, Project work and fieldwork for all faculties students. We are committed to prepare our students for a world we can’t envisage.
